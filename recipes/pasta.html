Pasta Salad

Description:

Salad dish prepared with one or more types of pasta, almost always chilled, and most often tossed in a vinegar, oil, or mayonnaise-based dressing. It is typically served as an appetizer, side dish or a main course.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain the pasta
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
